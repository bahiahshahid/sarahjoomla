About Me

Hello! I'm Siti Sarah Badli, a final-year student in the Bachelor of Information Science (Hons.) in Information Content Management program at UiTM Puncak Perdana. I am passionate about digital content, information management, and creating meaningful user experiences through well-organised and appealing web content.

My Background

In 2015 and 2019, studying at As-Sofa, a private religious school, was a meaningful experience. I completed my 30-juz hafazan and passed SPM through their accelerated program. The journey, with its highs and lows, shaped who I am today—and for that, I'm truly grateful.

In 2022, I began my journey at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), where I gained valuable knowledge in Information Management (IM110). Through academic and extracurricular involvement, I grew both professionally and personally. The supportive environment and dedicated lecturers shaped my success and lasting friendships—UiTM played a key role in my journey.

What I Have Learned

Through my degree studies, I have learned various technical and soft skills related to content management and digital publishing. This includes:

  • Creating and managing websites using Joomla, Wix, and other CMS tools
  • Understanding metadata, digital preservation, and information architecture
  • Basic PHP & MySQL database integration for user systems
  • Using tools like Conifer for web archiving and GitHub for version control
  • Writing, designing, and managing information for different digital platforms

These experiences have helped me develop confidence in presenting digital content that is not just informative but also visually appealing and user-friendly.
